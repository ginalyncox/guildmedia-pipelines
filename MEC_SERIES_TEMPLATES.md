# MEC Series Templates — Copy & Paste

Ready-to-paste **event titles**, **Zoom meeting topics**, and **description HTML** for each
Ganjier Guild replay series. Copy into MEC when creating or updating an event.

**Before pasting:** confirm Zoom links are still current. Update any `{placeholder}` values.

**Naming rules:** see [`MEC_EVENT_STANDARD.md`](MEC_EVENT_STANDARD.md).

---

## Quick reference

| Series | MEC event title | Zoom meeting topic | Category | Schedule |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| All Hands On Deck | `Ganjier Guild – All Hands On Deck` | `All Hands On Deck` | All Hands On Deck | Fridays 11:00 AM–12:00 PM CT |
| Farm Focus | `Ganjier Guild – Farm Focus` | `Farm Focus` | Farm Focus | Thursdays 7:00–9:00 PM CT (biweekly) |
| Open Session | `Ganjier Guild – Open Session` | `Open Session` | Open Sessions | `{day}` `{time}` CT |
| Knowledge Building | `Ganjier Guild – Knowledge Building` | `Knowledge Building` | Knowledge Building | Mondays 1:00–3:00 PM CT |
| State of the Guild | `Ganjier Guild – State of the Guild` | `State of the Guild` | State of the Guild | `{monthly day}` `{time}` CT |
| Navigator Session | `Ganjier Guild – Navigator Session` | `Navigator Session` | Community Education | `{day}` `{time}` CT |
| Special / one-off | `Ganjier Guild – {Session Topic}` | `{Session Topic}` | Special Sessions | Per event |

## 4. Knowledge Building

**MEC event title** (use the short form for recurring events)

```
Ganjier Guild – Knowledge Building
```

For a themed arc, you may extend the title — but keep **Knowledge Building** in the
Zoom topic for matching:

```
Ganjier Guild – Knowledge Building: Genetics and Cultivation
```

**Zoom meeting topic** (always include the series name)

```
Knowledge Building
```

**Canva thumbnail title (example)**

```
Knowledge Building — June 2026
```

**Description (paste into MEC)**

```html
<p><strong>Knowledge Building</strong><br />
A Ganjier Guild Community Education series</p>

<p><strong>Schedule</strong><br />
Mondays | 1:00–3:00 PM CT | Via Zoom</p>

<p><strong>Join live</strong><br />
<a href="https://zoom.us/j/97283928834">Join on Zoom</a><br />
Meeting ID: 972 8392 8834</p>

<p><strong>About this session</strong><br />
The Knowledge Building series explores cannabis genetics and cultivation through a
past, present, and future lens. Together we examine the history of the plant, the
decisions that shaped modern genetics, and how cultivation practices evolved alongside
underground markets, commercialization, and legalization.</p>

<p>Grounded in historical context and shared language, this series is designed to
develop informed professionals who can engage thoughtfully, advocate effectively, and
help steward the future of sustainable craft cannabis.</p>

<p><strong>Who should attend</strong><br />
Certified Ganjiers, Ganjier students, cannabis professionals, and any Guild member
curious about the science and story behind the plant.</p>

<p>Questions? Contact <a href="mailto:navigators@ganjierguild.com">navigators@ganjierguild.com</a>.</p>

<p><em>Replay: published automatically after the session ends. A replay link will
appear on this event page.</em></p>
```

## 7. Special / one-off session

For conferences, guest speakers, after-parties, or single-occurrence events.

**MEC event title**

```
Ganjier Guild – {Session Topic}
```

**Zoom meeting topic** (must match the distinctive part of the title)

```
{Session Topic}
```

**Example — NECANN weekend (in-person, no replay)**

```
Ganjier Guild @ NeCann Boston April 24-26, 2026
```

**Description (paste into MEC)** — for recorded Zoom specials only

```html
<p><strong>{Session Topic}</strong><br />
{One-line tagline}</p>

<p><strong>Schedule</strong><br />
{DATE_OR_RANGE} | {START_TIME}–{END_TIME} CT | Via Zoom</p>

<p><strong>Join live</strong><br />
<a href="{ZOOM_JOIN_URL}">Join on Zoom</a><br />
Meeting ID: {MEETING_ID}</p>

<p><strong>About this session</strong><br />
{2–4 sentences describing the session, audience, and what members will take away.}</p>

<p><em>Replay: published automatically after the session ends. A replay link will
appear on this event page.</em></p>
```

Skip the replay note for in-person-only events (no Zoom recording).

## After you paste

1. Set MEC **start/end datetime** to the actual session (America/Chicago).
2. Assign the **category** from the quick-reference table.
3. Confirm the Zoom **meeting topic** matches before going live.
4. After the session, verify **Replay available** on the event page.
